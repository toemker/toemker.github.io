Current project

In close collabaration with Nephological semantics project, I use vector space models to look at the semasiological variation within a type (e.g. polysemy) and a visualization tool developed within the project to assess the effect of parameter tuning in our models. Finaly, my goal is to reconstruct a real world semantic distribution, as found in a dictionary, by means of distributional semantics.
